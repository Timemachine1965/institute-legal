Privacy Policy

Institute Production SMS · Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Institute ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our SMS messaging program ("Institute Production SMS"). This policy applies only to SMS messages sent and received through our internal workspace platform at workspace.institute.pictures.

Who We Are

Institute is a creative company operating as Institute Studios Ltd. Our work spans film and commercial production and artist management. Our SMS program is a closed, internal communications tool used exclusively by our authorized employees and contractors for production-related operations.

Information We Collect

For the SMS program, we collect and store the following information from authorized users:

  • Mobile phone number (provided by the user during onboarding)
  • Name
  • Workspace account information (role, project assignments)
  • Message content and timestamps related to production operations (purchase orders, invoices, crew coordination, vendor approvals, and similar)

How We Use Information

Phone numbers and SMS content are used solely to operate our internal production workflow. Specifically:

  • Sending invoice intake confirmations and purchase order approval requests
  • Crew call notifications and scheduling updates
  • Vendor approval requests
  • Production-related operational coordination
  • Responding to user-initiated commands (HELP, STOP)

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers and SMS content are not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any external party for promotional use.

We use Twilio Inc. as our SMS infrastructure provider. Twilio processes message delivery on our behalf under their standard data processing terms. No other third parties receive SMS data.

Marketing

We do not send marketing or promotional SMS messages. All messages are operational and tied to active production work.

Data Retention

SMS message content is retained for the duration of the relevant production project plus a reasonable record-keeping period as required by accounting and legal obligations. Phone numbers are retained while the user remains an authorized workspace participant and are removed when access is revoked.

Your Rights

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Replying HELP returns support contact information. To request deletion of your phone number from our system or to access information we hold about you, contact us at the address below.

Security

Phone numbers and message content are stored on secured infrastructure with access limited to authorized personnel. We use industry-standard practices to protect data in transit and at rest.

Children

This SMS program is for authorized adult employees and contractors only. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

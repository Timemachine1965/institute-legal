Program Name

Institute Production SMS

Program Description

Institute Production SMS is a two-way SMS workflow tool operated by Institute (Institute Studios Ltd) for our internal film and commercial production operations. The program is used exclusively by authorized employees and contractors of Institute who participate in the company's internal workspace platform at workspace.institute.pictures. This is a closed program with no public sign-up.

Types of Messages

You will receive transactional and operational SMS messages related to active production work, including:

  • Invoice intake confirmations
  • Purchase order approval requests
  • Crew call notifications and scheduling updates
  • Vendor approval requests
  • Production operational coordination
  • Responses to your HELP and STOP commands

We do not send marketing or promotional SMS messages.

Message Frequency

You will receive up to 10 messages per month per user, on average. Frequency may vary depending on the production phase and your role on active projects.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please consult your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number to Institute during workspace onboarding and confirming via your onboarding email, you consent to receive SMS messages from Institute Production SMS at the number you provided. Consent is given individually by each user; administrators do not opt in on behalf of users.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time. Reply STOP to any message you receive from Institute Production SMS to unsubscribe. After replying STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message and will not receive further messages from the program.

Help

For help or support, reply HELP to any message you receive from Institute Production SMS, or contact us at support@instituteartist.com.
